**App Name:** Tic-Tac-Toe
**Developer:** Thia Solutions LLC
**Effective Date:** April 9, 2026
**Last Updated:** April 9, 2026

## Overview

Tic-Tac-Toe ("the App") is developed and maintained by Thia Solutions LLC ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information is accessed when you use the App, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. The App is designed to collect as little data as possible.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal information through the App.

### Game Center Data (via Apple)

The App uses **Apple Game Center**, Apple's social gaming platform, to provide the following features:

- Online multiplayer matchmaking
- Leaderboards (lifetime win count)

When you use these features, Apple Game Center provides the App with:

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Game Center Player ID | Identify players within a match session |
| Display Name | Show your name to your opponent during a match |
| Leaderboard score (win count) | Display rankings on Game Center leaderboard |

This data is provided by Apple and governed entirely by **Apple's Privacy Policy**. We do not store, log, or transmit this data to any server of our own.

---

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

- Name, email address, or contact information
- Location data (GPS or IP-based)
- Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV)
- Camera, microphone, or photo library data
- Health or financial data
- Browsing history or behavioural tracking data
- Crash logs or analytics (no third-party analytics SDK is used)

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App uses **Apple Game Center** as its sole third-party service.

| Service | Provider | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|---|
| Game Center | Apple Inc. | Matchmaking, leaderboards, player identity | [apple.com/legal/privacy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) |

No other third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or data brokers are integrated into the App.

---

## 4. Data Storage & Retention

- The App stores **no user data** on external servers — we operate no backend infrastructure.
- All game state (board, score, turn) exists only in device memory during an active session and is discarded when the session ends.
- Leaderboard scores are stored and managed entirely by Apple Game Center.
- Local notifications (used to remind you of your turn when the app is backgrounded) are generated on-device and are never transmitted externally.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

The App is rated **4+** on the Apple App Store and is suitable for all ages.

- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
- The App does not contain advertising, in-app purchases, or any mechanism that targets children.
- Game Center account creation and age verification are handled entirely by Apple.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us at the address below and we will take immediate steps to address it.

---

## 6. Local Notifications

The App may send **local notifications** to remind you when it is your turn during a multiplayer game. These notifications are:

- Generated entirely on your device
- Never transmitted to or stored on any external server
- Controlled by you — you can disable them at any time in **iOS Settings → Notifications → Tic-Tac-Toe**
